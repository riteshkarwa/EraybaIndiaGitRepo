How to Use

  1. Wash hair with detox shampoo and towel dry.
  2. Divide hair into sections, apply treatment root to ends.
  3. Exposure: 30′ for voluminous/frizzy; 60′ for curly hair.
  4. Rinse, blow-dry, then flat iron each strand (Temperature: 180-200 °C for fragile/bleached / 220-240 °C for normal hair).
  5. Use aftercare BIOsmooth maintenance kit for longer results. Wear gloves. Avoid contact with sensitive areas.
