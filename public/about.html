Our Philosophy

Professional Hair Cosmetics for Salons and Stylists

Since the foundation of the company back in 1983, Erayba has always kept in mind its origins: salons and professional hairdressers. Our dream keeps going on all around the world providing professional hair cosmetics and cutting-edge solutions to stylists and colorists, helping professionals to unleash creativity and achieve the best results to create true beauty.

Think Hair. Think Beauty.

We share the passion with professional hairdressers: beauty.

We love what we do, and are committed to deliver the best quality products with the highest professional performance, using unique up-to-date formulations and the best natural and vegan ingredients, to treat the hair in the healthiest way with deep care and respect, but also respecting our environment.

A Better World Through Beauty.

At Erayba, we have the mission to foster wellbeing and bring happiness to our communities through beauty. We support and encourage hairdressers, colorists, educators, stylists and creative minds to achieve true beauty.

We believe a better world is possible through beauty in perfect harmony with nature.
